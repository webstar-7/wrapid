About Us

We are a professional employer organisation making it fast and easy to employ the most talented in no time.

We take care of payroll, benefits, compliance, taxes, and admin so you can focus on growing your business.

At Wrapid we match adventurous jobseekers to esteemed companies. Our goal is to connect as many talents and to give everyone a chance to bring posibilities to life. At this moment we have talented jobseekers in various fields.

  • The recruitment process can start. We will share your vacancy amongst the thousands of jobseekers on our job platform. Furthermore, we will share your job openings on our Social Media channels. Our power lies in the fact that we reach thousands of people every week.
  • Next, we will generate applicants and perform a pre-screening if you like. We provide the right information and help every candidate during the process. This leads to you finding the perfect match for your business!
  • The process is easy. Start by filling in the contact form below. We will contact you shortly to discuss the vacancy and recruitment possibilities.
